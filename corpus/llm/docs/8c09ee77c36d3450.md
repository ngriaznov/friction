**Quillfeather Keyboard Shortcuts**

As you dive into the world of terminal-based text editing with Quillfeather, getting familiar with its keyboard shortcuts will save you a ton of time and effort. Here's a comprehensive list of shortcuts grouped by category to help you navigate, edit, search, and manage your windows like a pro.

**Navigation**

* **Move cursor:** `Ctrl + A` (beginning of line), `Ctrl + E` (end of line), `Ctrl + F` (forward one character), `Ctrl + B` (backward one character)
* **Jump to:** `Ctrl + O` (next line with matching tag), `Ctrl + U` (previous line with matching tag)
* **Scrolling:** `Shift + Page Up` (scroll up a page), `Shift + Page Down` (scroll down a page)

**Editing**

* **Basic actions:** `Ctrl + C` (copy selection), `Ctrl + V` (paste), `Ctrl + Z` (undo), `Ctrl + Y` (redo)
* **Cut, copy, and paste:** `Ctrl + X` (cut selection), `Ctrl + Shift + V` (paste without formatting)
* **Text manipulation:** `Ctrl + K` (delete to end of line), `Ctrl + L` (center text), `Ctrl + R` (replace word)

**Search**

* **Find and replace:** `Ctrl + F` (find), `Ctrl + H` (replace), `Ctrl + Shift + F` (find in files)
* **Navigation by search results:** `Ctrl + G` (go to next match), `Ctrl + Shift + G` (go to previous match)

**Window Management**

* **New window:** `Ctrl + N` (new window with current file)
* **Close window:** `Ctrl + W` (close current window)
* **Arrange windows:** `Ctrl + Shift + A` (tile horizontally), `Ctrl + Shift + S` (tile vertically)

Some additional tips to keep in mind:

* Quillfeather uses the standard Emacs-style keyboard shortcuts. If you're already familiar with them, you'll feel right at home.
* Many of these shortcuts have equivalents using the mouse or GUI menus, but using keyboard shortcuts will save you time and improve your overall productivity.
* You can customize some of these shortcuts to suit your preferences by checking out Quillfeather's configuration files.

With this comprehensive list of keyboard shortcuts, you'll be well on your way to becoming a Quillfeather master. Practice makes perfect – give them a try and see how much time and effort they save you!
