**A Quarter of Service**

In Q3 (July-September), we distributed an astonishing 250,000 meals to those in need! This is a testament to our collective commitment to providing sustenance and dignity to our neighbors. We are proud to report that this number represents:

* A 15% increase in meal distribution compared to the same period last year
* Over 4,500 households served, with an average of 55 meals per household
* Food bank clients citing improved health outcomes, reduced stress, and increased energy due to our services

We are honored to be a part of this vital support system for our community. Our food bank's mission is more than just providing sustenance – it's about empowering individuals to thrive.

**Spotlight: New Mobile Distribution Route**

This quarter, we launched our new mobile distribution route in the Oakwood neighborhood! Led by volunteers like you, this initiative brings fresh produce and essential groceries directly to residents who may face transportation barriers or limited access to traditional food assistance programs. Our mobile pantry serves:

* 75 households per month
* An average of 200 pounds of fresh produce and dry goods distributed each week

This innovative program is made possible by your contributions, volunteer time, and generosity. We are excited to expand our reach and continue serving this vibrant community.

**Call to Action: Canned Goods Drive**

As the holiday season approaches, we rely heavily on donations to stock our shelves and meet the growing demand for food assistance. Your support is crucial in helping us ensure that no one goes hungry during this time of year. We kindly request that you consider donating canned goods, such as:

* Vegetables (beans, corn, peas)
* Fruits (fruits, fruit juices)
* Proteins (tuna, beans, peanut butter)
* Soups and broths
* Canned meats and fish

Your generosity will help us provide over 500,000 meals during the holiday season. Collection bins are located in our warehouse lobby, and you can also drop off donations at local participating businesses.
