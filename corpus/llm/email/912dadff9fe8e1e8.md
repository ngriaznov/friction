Subject: Update to Parental Leave Policy

Dear All,

As part of our ongoing efforts to support employees and their families, we are excited to announce an update to our parental leave policy. This change aims to provide more comprehensive benefits and flexibility for new parents, while also ensuring that our company remains competitive in the market.

**New Duration:**

The updated policy now provides up to 12 weeks of paid parental leave for eligible employees. This increase is designed to support new parents during a critical period when they need time to care for their newborn or adopted child.

**Eligibility Rules:**

To be eligible for the new parental leave policy, employees must:

* Have completed at least one year of service with the company
* Be employed full-time (30 hours/week) or part-time (20 hours/week)
* Have a qualifying birth or adoption experience

The updated policy also includes provisions for adoptive parents and same-sex partners. If you have questions about your eligibility, please reach out to our HR team.

**Interaction with Short-Term Disability:**

For employees who are also receiving short-term disability benefits, the new parental leave policy will be coordinated with their existing coverage. In most cases, this means that an employee's short-term disability benefit will be reduced or suspended during their parental leave period. However, some exceptions may apply, and our HR team will work closely with you to ensure a smooth transition.

**Scheduling a Call:**

We understand that this change may raise questions for many of you. If you have personal questions or concerns about the updated policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to our HR team. We are here to support you and provide guidance on how the new policy will impact your benefits.

To schedule a call with one of our HR representatives, simply reply to this email with your name, department, and preferred date/time for the call. Our team will work with you to find a convenient time that suits your needs.

**Additional Resources:**

For more information about our updated parental leave policy, please visit our company intranet page under "Benefits" or contact our HR team directly.

We are committed to supporting our employees and their families during this exciting time. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

Thank you for your understanding, and we look forward to supporting you on this new journey.
