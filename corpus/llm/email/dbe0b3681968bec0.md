Subject: Important Update: Revision to Our Parental Leave Policy

Dear Valued Employees,

We hope this message finds you well. We are writing to inform you of an important update to our company’s parental leave policy, effective immediately. This revision aims to better support our families and reflect the evolving needs of our workforce.

**New Duration:** Effective from July 1, 2024, employees will be eligible for up to **16 weeks** of paid parental leave. This increase is designed to provide greater flexibility and support for new parents during this critical time.

**Eligibility Rules:**
- Eligible employees must have been with the company for at least **12 months** on the date of their request.
- Both biological and adoptive parents are eligible, as well as legal guardians who take care of a child in their home or adopt a child from any part of the world.
- The leave can be taken consecutively or intermittently, depending on family needs and employer discretion.

**Interaction with Short-Term Disability (STD):**
The new parental leave policy is designed to complement our existing Short-Term Disability (STD) benefits. If an employee takes advantage of both policies within a 12-month period, the total paid time off will not exceed **24 weeks**. This ensures that employees can take full advantage of these benefits without overutilizing either program.

**Steps for Requesting Leave:**
1. Submit a formal request through our HR portal at least **60 days** prior to your anticipated leave start date.
2. Provide documentation supporting eligibility (e.g., employment verification, birth certificate).
3. Your manager will review the request and confirm approval if all criteria are met.
