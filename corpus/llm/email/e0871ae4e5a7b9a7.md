Subject: Important Update to Parental Leave Policy

Dear Team,

I hope this message finds you well. I am writing to inform you about an update to our company's parental leave policy. As we continue to support and value the diverse needs of our employees, we are making some changes that will better accommodate new parents while ensuring consistency in our benefits package.

Effective immediately, the new parental leave policy is as follows:

### Duration
- **Primary Parent:** 12 weeks of paid leave (up from 8 weeks previously).
- **Secondary Parent:** 6 weeks of paid leave (unchanged).

### Eligibility Rules
To be eligible for this leave, employees must:
- Have been with the company for at least six months prior to the expected due date.
- Be working in a full-time capacity.
- Provide a signed medical certificate or other relevant documentation from their healthcare provider.

### Interaction with Short-Term Disability
If an employee is also eligible for short-term disability benefits, they can utilize both. The total duration of leave will be extended by any days covered under short-term disability, up to the maximum stated above (primary parent: 12 weeks; secondary parent: 6 weeks). This means that in cases where both conditions apply, a primary parent could potentially take up to 18 weeks of combined leave.
