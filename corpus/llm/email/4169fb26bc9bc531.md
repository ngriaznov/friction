Subject: Announcing Our Move to a Self-Hosted MkDocs Wiki!

Hi Team,

I'm excited to announce that we'll be transitioning our internal wiki from Confluence to a self-hosted MkDocs setup over the next six weeks! This change comes after careful consideration and is driven by several key factors:

**Why MkDocs?**

* **Cost Savings:**  Confluence's licensing costs have become increasingly burdensome, especially as our team grows. MkDocs offers a free and open-source solution that eliminates these recurring expenses.
* **Improved Search Quality:** While Confluence's search functionality has its strengths, MkDocs leverages modern search technologies, resulting in more accurate and relevant search results. This will significantly enhance your ability to find the information you need quickly and efficiently.
* **Git Integration for Version Control & Collaboration:**

MkDocs is seamlessly integrated with Git, allowing us to leverage version control for all wiki content. This means:

    * **Transparent History:** Track changes made to pages over time, understand who made them, and revert to previous versions if needed.
    * **Collaborative Editing:** Review and approve changes directly within Git, fostering a more collaborative and transparent editing process.
    * **Reduced Risk of Data Loss:**  Git's robust backup and recovery mechanisms ensure that your wiki content is always protected.

**Migration Timeline & Your Role:**

We've developed a phased migration plan over the next six weeks:

* **Week 1-2:** Announcement and initial training sessions on MkDocs.
* **Week 3-4:** Pilot testing of the new platform with select teams. Gather feedback and refine the setup.
* **Week 5-6:** Full wiki migration. We'll guide you through transferring your existing Confluence pages to MkDocs.

**What You Need to Do:**

* **Review Your Existing Pages:**  Take some time to review your current Confluence pages and consider any updates or reorganizations you'd like to make before the migration.
* **Attend Training Sessions:** We'll be hosting training sessions on using MkDocs. These sessions will cover the basics, advanced features, and best practices for contributing to the new wiki.

**Questions?**
