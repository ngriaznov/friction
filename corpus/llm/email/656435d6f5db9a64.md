Subject: Incident Summary: Payment Processing API Outage - February 10th, 2023

Dear Engineering Team,

We are writing to summarize the recent incident that affected our payment processing API, which resulted in a two-hour outage starting at 2:15 PM EST on February 10th, 2023. This email provides an overview of the timeline, root cause, customer impact, and follow-up action items.

**Detection and Mitigation Timeline:**

* 2:15 PM EST (Incident Start): Customers reported issues with payment processing.
* 2:20 PM EST (Initial Investigation): Our monitoring team notified our incident response team.
* 2:25 PM EST (Root Cause Analysis): We determined that the issue was related to a misconfigured connection pool limit after the recent deployment.
* 2:45 PM EST (Fix Applied): The fix was applied, and the API was restarted.

**Root Cause:**

The root cause of the outage was a misconfigured connection pool limit in our payment processing API. After a recent deployment, the configuration was not updated to reflect the new requirements, leading to an excessive number of connections being opened, causing the system to become overwhelmed.

**Customer Impact:**

Our customers were affected for approximately two hours, resulting in some delayed transactions and potential losses. We have apologized for the inconvenience and are working to prevent similar incidents in the future.

**Follow-up Action Items:**

1. **Config Review**: Conduct a thorough review of our connection pool configuration and ensure that all applications are updated with the latest requirements. (Due: February 15th, 2023)
2. **Automated Monitoring**: Implement automated monitoring for connection pool limits to detect similar issues early on. (Due: February 22nd, 2023)
3. **Deployment Script Update**: Review and update our deployment scripts to ensure that all necessary configurations are applied during deployments. (Due: March 1st, 2023)

We apologize for the inconvenience caused by this incident and appreciate your prompt attention to these follow-up action items.
